Who Are We?

Obsessed with learning, we’re on a mission to liberate human potential. It starts with a radically new way of understanding and communicating talent... what organizations need, what professionals offer, and how it can be cultivated.

Atop this new paradigm we are building a set of modern tools that help professionals better define and share their talent, that help organizations better identify the talent they need and cultivate the talent they have, and help learning providers build products for today’s (and tomorrow’s) workforce.
