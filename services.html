What We Offer

CultivateMe provides software-driven consulting that works side-by-side with your teams, much like a Director of Learning as-a-service, to build a learning ecosystem that engages employees and evolves your business.

Role Analysis & Talent Mapping

Clarify the roles at your organization. Map your team’s capabilities against those roles. Develop personal learning plans.

Role Design & Visualization

Discover a more granular way of mapping roles and souls. Examine the activities and abilities that drive your work. Align them with the most appropriate talent.

Integrate new hires fast. Build strong team relationships. Make knowledge and skills more visible and accessible across the company.

DDO Design

Become a Deliberately Developmental Organization. Make talent development a core part of your culture. Watch your people flourish and your business transform.

Engagement
Model

While we tailor every engagement to our clients' needs, our work generally unfolds in the following stages:

  1. Explore

    Our goal is to understand the current state of learning in your organization. This typically involves 1:1 interviews, role design workshops, and a learning culture assessment. Then we design a set of practices and tools for use in the next stage.

  2. Prototype

    Here we test those practices and tools with a single team and tweak it as needed. We work with real people and roles, using retrospectives and direct engagement to gather feedback and make adjustments. The outcome is a more refined pathway for growth, plus a team to help scale the system in the following stage.

  3. Refine

    At this point we scale this system using a “mitosis” approach: each team trains another team. This make the scaling process exponential (2 teams, then 4, then 8, etc.) Training others deepens their understanding of the system. And the system becomes an internal collaboration rather than an external solution. Throughout we support teams, report on progress, and measure engagement.

  4. Calibrate

    As your people settle into the tools and practices, we gradually release responsibility for the learning culture. This includes weekly check-ins with internal champions, triage meetings, and minor system adjustments. The final result is a clear pathway for growth in your organization. One that is built on a culture of learning that engages, retains, and develops your people.

How We Work

  • Weekly Sprints: We define the work on Monday and we ship on Friday, week in and week out. All our work is tracked in Trello and completely transparent to you. You know exactly what’s happening at any time.
  • Gradual Release Of Responsibility: We design self-sustaining systems, i.e. we work ourselves out of a job. As the learning system and culture take hold we gradually remove ourselves and build capacity with internal champions.
