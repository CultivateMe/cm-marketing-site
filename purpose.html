Mission

We help businesses become learning organizations and turn work into a platform for self-development. People are their best selves when learning, and when people are their best selves, the business prospers.

Beliefs

We strongly believe developing your people and developing your business are one and the same. Here's why:

People want to learn. As human beings we are most alive when we are at that uncomfortable and exciting edge of not quite knowing what we are doing. We want to be pushed. We want to grow. Our deepest engagement happens when we are consciously and deliberately becoming better versions of ourselves.

Experience is the best teacher. No one learns from abstraction… from a bunch of words. Rather, we learn from having experiences and then reflecting on those experiences to make meaning from them. This is how we internalize information and change our behavior. This is how we create our minds and ourselves.

Work provides experience. We spend the majority of our waking hours working, making it the single largest provider of experience in our lives. When we reflect on our work more deeply and use it as the source of learning and growth, we become more engaged in our work. We see it as the pathway to forging a better self and we take it more seriously.

Learning is no longer optional. Business and markets change faster than ever. If your organization can’t learn, adapt, and evolve then it’s destined to become obsolete. Companies winning in today's markets understand they must “hard-wire” learning into their culture and systems.
